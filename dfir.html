Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR)

DFIR is a field focused on identifying, investigating, and recovering from security breaches and cyberattacks. It combines two crucial aspects:

  • Digital Forensics: The collection, preservation, and analysis of digital evidence following an incident.
  • Incident Response: The processes followed to handle a cybersecurity incident, mitigate damage, and restore normal operations.

Importance of DFIR

DFIR is crucial for effectively responding to security breaches, identifying the root cause of an attack, and preventing future incidents.
