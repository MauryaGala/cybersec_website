Linux for Cybersecurity

Linux is a widely-used open-source operating system, favored by cybersecurity professionals for its security, flexibility, and extensive range of security tools.

Why Linux is Essential for Cybersecurity

  • Open-Source: Allows full control over security settings and configurations.
  • Security Tools: Linux distributions like Kali Linux and Parrot OS offer a wide range of pre-installed cybersecurity tools.
  • Stable and Lightweight: Linux systems are known for their stability and resource efficiency, making them ideal for running security applications.
